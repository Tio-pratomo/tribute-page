Iwan Fals Profile

Virgiawan Listanto, known by his stage name Iwan Fals (born in Jakarta, 3 September 1961; age 58 years) is a singer, musician, songwriter, and critic who has become a legend in Indonesia. His musical style has been said to be pop, rock, country, and folk pop and his lyrics tell a lot about the dark days of the 1970s to 1980s in politics.

Achievement

  • The best selling, album Mata Dewa, BASF, 1988 - 1989.
  • Penyanyi rekaman pria terbaik, album Anak Wayang, BASF Award XI, 18 April 1996.

  • Video klip terbaik lagu Entah, Video Musik Indonesia periode VIII - 2000/2001.
